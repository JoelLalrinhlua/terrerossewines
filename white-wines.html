White Wine · Friuli DOC Grave

Chardonnay

The international ambassador of Terre Rosse, this Chardonnay captures the remarkable freshness that defines Friulian viticulture. Vinified in stainless steel to preserve its natural aromatics, this wine speaks of the estate's cool night temperatures and mineral-rich soils — a wine of immediacy and charm that balances Burgundian elegance with unmistakably Italian character.

02
White Wine · Native Variety

Friulano

Once called Tocai Friulano, this indigenous grape is the beating heart of Friulian wine culture. At Terre Rosse, it finds its most authentic expression: dry, mineral and gently bitter at the finish — a wine that has accompanied the tables of this region for centuries. Pair it with the legendary Prosciutto di San Daniele for an experience that is purely, joyfully Friulian.

03
White Wine · Friuli DOC Grave

Pinot Grigio

Far removed from the mass-produced versions that flooded international markets, the Terre Rosse Pinot Grigio is a wine of genuine Friulian character and substance. The cold nights of the Grave plains lock aromatic freshness into each grape, producing a wine of vibrant acidity, delicate mineral tension, and a finish that lingers with pleasant precision. A wine of honest, unpretentious pleasure.

Sauvignon

Intensely aromatic and vibrantly expressive, the Terre Rosse Sauvignon is a wine that announces itself with exuberant confidence. Friuli's cool continental climate is the ideal cradle for this variety — extending the growing season and locking in those characteristic green and tropical aromas that make each glass an olfactory journey. Bright, precise, and thoroughly alive.

05
White Wine · Gewürztraminer

Traminer Aromatico

Among the most opulently perfumed white wines produced in Friuli, the Traminer Aromatico of Terre Rosse is a wine of extraordinary floral and spiced intensity. Rose petals, lychee, and exotic fruits weave together in a bouquet of near-impossible abundance. On the palate, richness is tempered by Friuli's natural acidity, creating a wine of genuine complexity and extraordinary aromatic persistence.

06
White Wine · Ancient Friulian Variety

Ribolla Gialla

One of Friuli's oldest and most celebrated native varieties, Ribolla Gialla is experiencing a magnificent renaissance — and at Terre Rosse, it is handled with the reverence it deserves. This ancient vine produces wines of vivid citrus character, zesty acidity, and a refreshing mineral tension that speaks directly of its home. Clean, bright, and imbued with Friulian identity, Ribolla Gialla is the perfect introduction to the region's extraordinary white wine heritage.
